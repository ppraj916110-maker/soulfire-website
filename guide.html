Rich Dad's Guide to Investing

Lesson 1: Investing Is Not About Money

True investing starts with mindset and education, not money. Money follows knowledge.

Lesson 2: Why Invest?

Investing is the path to financial freedom; a job alone cannot build long-term wealth.

Lesson 3: Choose Your Type of Investor

Three types: Accredited, Sophisticated, and Inside Investors. Aim to be sophisticated or inside.

Lesson 4: The Right Mindset for Investing

Rich think in terms of creating assets; poor and middle-class think of job security.

Lesson 5: Build Your Business First

Create a solid foundation—business systems, financial statements, cash flow—before investing.

Lesson 6: It’s About the Plan, Not the Product

A good strategy matters more than chasing “hot investments.”

Lesson 7: Be the Bank, Not the Borrower

Position yourself to provide capital and earn returns, not pay interest.

Lesson 8: Control Over Investment

Don’t hand money blindly to managers. Learn to understand and manage investments.

Lesson 9: Minimize Risk Through Education

Investing is risky without education. Knowledge reduces risk and increases returns.

Lesson 10: The Ultimate Investor – Create Opportunities

The richest investors create businesses, real estate projects, or financial instruments.

Ready to Take action

Start listing your assets & liabilities this week. Aim to add one small income-generating asset in 90 days.
