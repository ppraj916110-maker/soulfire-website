12 Rules for Life

12 Rules for Life by Jordan B. Peterson is a thought-provoking guide that blends psychology, philosophy, and practical wisdom. It offers timeless principles to bring meaning, order, and responsibility into our lives.

Key Lessons from the Book

Rule 1: Stand Up Straight with Your Shoulders Back

Posture reflects confidence. Carry yourself with strength to signal self-respect and resilience.

Rule 2: Treat Yourself Like Someone You Are Responsible for Helping

Show yourself the same care, discipline, and compassion that you would extend to a loved one.

Rule 3: Make Friends with People Who Want the Best for You

Surround yourself with positive, supportive people who uplift and encourage your growth.

Rule 4: Compare Yourself to Who You Were Yesterday, Not to Who Someone Else is Today

Progress is personal. Focus on your own improvement instead of competing with others.

Rule 5: Do Not Let Your Children Do Anything That Makes You Dislike Them

Parenting with discipline and boundaries helps children grow into responsible, respectful adults.

Rule 6: Set Your House in Perfect Order Before You Criticize the World

Before blaming society, fix your personal habits, responsibilities, and environment.

Rule 7: Pursue What is Meaningful, Not What is Expedient

Meaning requires responsibility and sacrifice, while expedience focuses only on short-term gains.
