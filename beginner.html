Beginner Stock Market Course

A beginner's course on the stock market is designed to provide you with the fundamental knowledge and skills needed to start investing.

Key Concepts You'll Learn

  • Introduction to Investing: Saving vs investing, risk vs reward.
  • Understanding the Stock Market: NSE, BSE, IPO, primary/secondary market.
  • Key Terminology: Bull/bear market, dividends, IPOs.
  • Types of Investments: Stocks, mutual funds, ETFs.
  • Analysis Methods: Fundamental & technical analysis.
  • Setting Up Your Account: Opening a Demat and trading account.
  • Building a Strategy: Goals, risk tolerance, diversification.
  • Risk Management: Avoiding common mistakes.

Finding a Course

  • Free Platforms: Zerodha Varsity, Angel One, Great Learning.
  • Universities: Yale, Michigan on Coursera.
  • Online Platforms: Udemy, YouTube, etc.
  • Broker Content: Webinars, tutorials from brokers.

Before You Begin

  1. Set Clear Goals: Know your why.
  2. Understand Your Risk Tolerance: Be realistic.
  3. Practice with a Simulator: Try virtual trading.
  4. Start Small: Use money you can afford to lose.
