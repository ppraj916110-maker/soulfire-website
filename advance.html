Mastering the Markets: An Advanced Trading Course

This comprehensive course is designed for intermediate to advanced traders who want to deepen their understanding of financial markets and develop a sophisticated trading strategy...

  1. Foundations of Advanced Trading

    • Review of Core Concepts:

      A quick refresh on market types, order types, and basic risk management.

    • Building a Trading Plan:

      The importance of a detailed trading journal, backtesting strategies, and psychological discipline.

    • Choosing Your Trading Style:

      How to select a style that fits your personality, capital, and time commitment.

  2. Advanced Trading Types & Strategies

    • Day Trading Mastery:

      A deep dive into Scalping vs. Day Trading, high-frequency trading concepts, and order flow analysis.

    • Swing Trading & Position Trading:

      Identifying trends on higher timeframes and managing overnight risk.

    • Algorithmic Trading:

      Introduction to automated systems.

    • Options Trading:

      Advanced option strategies and "The Greeks."

    • Futures & Commodities Trading:

      Contract specifications and market dynamics.

    • Forex Trading:

      Advanced currency correlation and carry trades.

  3. Advanced Technical Indicators

    Oscillators & Momentum Indicators

    • Relative Strength Index (RSI) & MACD:

      A deep dive into these key oscillators.

    • Stochastic Oscillator:

      Analogy of two runners for buy/sell signals.

    Trend-Following & Volatility Indicators

    • Moving Averages:

      A review of SMA, EMA, and WMA.

    • Bollinger Bands:

      Used to measure market volatility and identify potential breakouts or reversals.

    • Average Directional Index (ADX)

    • Ichimoku Cloud

    Volume & Market Profile Indicators

    • Volume Analysis:

      Why volume matters, OBV, and volume confirmation for breakouts.

    • Volume Profile

    • Volume Weighted Average Price (VWAP)

  4. Advanced Chart Patterns

    • Continuation Patterns (Complex):

      Flags, Pennants, Rectangles, and Triangles.

    • Reversal Patterns (Advanced):

      Head and Shoulders, Double Top/Bottom, Rounding Bottom, etc.

    • Harmonic Patterns:

      Gartley, Bat, Butterfly, Crab.

    • Elliott Wave Theory:

      Overview.

    • Setting Entry & Exit Targets:

      Strategies for defining points based on patterns.

  5. Advanced Candlestick Patterns & Price Action

    Reversal & Continuation Patterns (Beyond the Basics)

    • Reversal Patterns:

      Morning Star, Evening Star, Three White Soldiers, Three Black Crows.

    • Continuation Patterns:

      Tasuki Gap, Mat Hold.

    Price Action Trading

    • Pin Bar

    • Doji

    • Engulfing

  6. Advanced Risk Management & Psychology

    • Risk Management:

      Stop-loss, take-profit, risk/reward, position sizing, trailing stops.

    • Trading Psychology:

      Discipline, avoiding emotional trading, journaling trades.
