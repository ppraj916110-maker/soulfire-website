Mastering the Markets: An Advanced Trading Course

This comprehensive course is designed for intermediate to advanced traders who want to deepen their understanding of financial markets and develop a sophisticated trading strategy.

0%

Completed: 0/8

  • Lesson 1: Foundations of Advanced Trading
    • Review of Core Concepts: A quick refresh on market types, order types, and basic risk management.
    • Building a Trading Plan: Importance of a trading journal, backtesting, and discipline.
    • Choosing Your Trading Style: Matching style to personality, capital, and time.
  • Lesson 2: Advanced Trading Types & Strategies
    • Day Trading Mastery: Scalping vs. Day Trading, HFT concepts, and order flow.
    • Swing & Position Trading: Trend identification and managing overnight risk.
    • Algorithmic Trading: Intro to automated systems.
    • Options Trading: Advanced strategies and "The Greeks."
    • Futures & Commodities: Contract specs and market dynamics.
    • Forex Trading: Currency correlation and carry trades.
  • Lesson 3: Advanced Technical Indicators
    • RSI & MACD deep dive.
    • Stochastic Oscillator explained.
    • Moving Averages (SMA, EMA, WMA).
    • Bollinger Bands, ADX, Ichimoku Cloud.
    • Volume Analysis, OBV, Volume Profile, VWAP.
  • Lesson 4: Advanced Chart Patterns
    • Continuation Patterns: Flags, Pennants, Rectangles, Triangles.
    • Reversal Patterns: Head & Shoulders, Double/Triple Tops, Wedges.
    • Harmonic Patterns: Gartley, Bat, Butterfly, Crab.
    • Elliott Wave Theory overview.
    • Setting Entry & Exit Targets.
  • Lesson 5: Advanced Candlestick Patterns & Price Action
    • Reversal Patterns: Morning Star, Evening Star, Three White Soldiers, Three Black Crows.
    • Continuation Patterns: Tasuki Gap, Mat Hold.
    • Price Action: Pin Bar, Doji, Engulfing.
  • Lesson 6: Advanced Risk Management
    • Stop-loss & take-profit levels.
    • Risk/reward ratio calculation.
    • Position sizing strategies (Kelly Criterion).
    • Trailing stops & scaling positions.
  • Lesson 7: Trading Psychology
    • Building discipline.
    • Avoiding emotional trading (FOMO, revenge trading).
    • Journaling trades for improvement.
  • Course Outcome
    • Formulate advanced trading strategies.
    • Identify complex indicators and patterns.
    • Use candlestick analysis for precision.
    • Combine multiple analysis methods.
    • Implement sophisticated risk management.
    • Maintain psychological discipline.
